Welcome!

CharEnGen is a free-to-use online character generator for Dungeons & Dragons Fifth Edition. This site is designed to help you generate a character quickly and easily with little to no hassle. Each page has general information on each race, class, and background found in the Player's Handbook, however, you will still need a copy of the Player's Handbook for rules and specific details.

With CharEnGen, creating a character is easy! Simply select a race, class, and background along with any other options like sub-race, and the character generator will generate a stats for your character.

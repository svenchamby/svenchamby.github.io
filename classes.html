Classes

In D&D, a class is like a character's profession. One might choose to devote their life to serving their patron god and become a cleric. Another might study magic as a wizard or be a mighty warrior from a barbarian tribe. Your character's class will be most important in determining what your character can do. Combat styles, the ability to cast spells, and your character's various skills will largely be derived from what attributes are afforded them by their class.

Each class is unique in its own way and each has a few subclasses that will help you build a unique character. The guide below will help you in determining which class you'd like to play. Just click on the name of the class that interests you and some general information will appear.

What to Avoid

Here's a list of 'Do-Nots' that search engines are programmed to avoid:

  • Too many Keywords
  • Too many Ads
  • Link Buying
  • Identical Content
  • Hidden Text or Links

As a rule of thumb, focus on quality content and don't try to cheat the system. People who work at Google are smarter than you.
